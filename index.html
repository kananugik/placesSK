Discover South Korea

South Korea, officially known as the Republic of Korea, is a beautiful country in East Asia. With its rich history, breathtaking landscapes and vibrant culture, South Korea offers a wealth of experiences for travellers.

One of the highlights of South Korea is its bustling capital, Seoul. This modern city seamlessly blends ancient traditions with modern wonders. Explore the historic palaces of Gyeongbokgung and Changdeokgung that reflect the grandeur of the Joseon Dynasty. Walk the busy streets of Myeongdong and Dongdaemun, where you can find shopping, delicious street food and vibrant nightlife.

Outside of Seoul, South Korea boasts breathtaking natural beauty. The state is adorned with picturesque mountains, serene valleys and pristine beaches. Visit Seoraksan National Park, where you can hike amid stunning mountains, waterfalls and vibrant autumn foliage. Explore the volcanic wonder of Jeju Island, with stunning beaches, cliffs and unique cliffs.
